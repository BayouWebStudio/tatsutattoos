About the Artist

Your Favorite Anime & Manga Brought to Life

Rene Vasquez — known as Tatsu — is a Houston-based tattoo artist at Tattoo Temple Houston specializing in anime, video game, and pop culture-inspired tattoos that bring your favorite characters and worlds to life in vibrant, permanent detail. With 7+ years of professional experience, Tatsu crafts every piece with meticulous attention to linework, color, and storytelling.

From One Piece and Pokémon to Dragon Ball and dark fantasy, Tatsu turns your fandom passion into museum-quality ink — custom pieces and flash both available. Flexible payment plans through Afterpay, Affirm, and Klarna make your dream tattoo accessible.

Award-winning: 1st Place at the Scottsdale Art Fusion 2023. Book your consultation today.
